The Team

Est. 2026. Embrained, LLC is a neurorobotics company dedicated to democratizing access to embodied AI.

Dr. Christopher Harris

Founder & CEO

Dr. Christopher Harris is a neuroscientist (PhD, Sussex) and former PI at Backyard Brains, where he created the educational SpikerBot platform.

He founded Embrained in 2026 to democratize embodied AI, designing architectures that allow standard PCs to drive complex robots. His long-term aim is to integrate essential biological primitives into the Embrained ecosystem to explore general intelligence and liveliness.

Watch: Emergence in Educational Neurorobotics

Dr. John Griffiths

CSO & Special Advisor

Dr. John Griffiths is Head of the Whole Brain Modelling Group at CAMH's Krembil Centre for Neuroinformatics and an Assistant Professor at the University of Toronto. He holds a PhD from the University of Cambridge.

His work bridges abstract computational models and biological reality, focusing on the mathematical modelling of large-scale neural dynamics.
