# Embrained Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 27, 2026

Embrained, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data collected through the use of the Plexus Beta Hardware and the Embrained Software Suite.

## 1. Local Processing First
The core architecture of Embrained is designed around **Privacy-Preserving Co-Training**. This means the Software Suite is designed to process your video streams locally on your hardware. 
- **Raw Camera Feeds:** The Plexus robot streams visual data exclusively to your local WiFi network. We do not automatically upload, capture, or store your raw camera feeds on our servers.
- **Topological Models:** The neural networks trained on your machine reside locally. 

## 2. Information We Collect
When you purchase a Plexus robot through our Shopify store, we collect standard e-commerce information:
- **Contact Information:** Name, email address, and phone number.
- **Shipping Information:** Your physical mailing address.
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely through Shopify and our banking partners; we do not store your full credit card number.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected during checkout exclusively to:
- Process and fulfill your Plexus robot order.
- Communicate with you regarding shipping updates, beta firmware patches, and critical software releases.
- Provide customer support during the Beta Pilot.

## 4. Community Data Sharing
If you participate in our Discord community or choose to share trained models:
- You are strictly sharing mathematical tensors/latents.
- We advise users not to share raw datasets publicly. Any data you voluntarily upload to our Discord server or HuggingFace repositories is done at your own risk.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at beta@embrained.ai.
